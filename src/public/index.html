Welcome to Fooder!

Fooder is an incredibly simple service that will help you find food trucks in San Francisco. It's (mostly) automatic if you allow access to your location; if you can't (or won't!), just search instead.

Usage: type in the box. Toggle with ESC.

Important note:
Autodetection only works if you are currently in San Francisco! Otherwise, use the available search box to narrow your search and peruse the beautiful map service from openstreetmap.org.

Let's go!
